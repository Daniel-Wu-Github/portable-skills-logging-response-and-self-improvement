| Dimension | 0 | 1 | 2 |
|---|---|---|---|
| Trigger quality | rarely loads for right task | sometimes loads | reliably loads |
| Scope fit | guidance too broad/narrow | mostly fits | tight fit |
| Outcome support | doesn't improve execution | helps somewhat | materially improves |
| Noise control | causes confusion | minor noise | clean, no noise |

**Thresholds:** 7–8 = keep; 4–6 = revise; 0–3 = rewrite or split
